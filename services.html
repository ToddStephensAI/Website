02 / Loft Conversions

Add Space, Value & a New Bedroom

A loft conversion is one of the most effective ways to add space and value to your home. Our structural specialists handle every type — from planning and building regulations through to the final fit-out, staircase and sign-off certification.

  • Dormer conversions — maximum headroom & usable space
  • Hip-to-gable conversions — ideal for semi-detached homes
  • Mansard conversions — the most spacious option available
  • Velux / roof light conversions — the most cost-effective route
  • Full architectural drawings & structural calculations
  • Building regulations sign-off included as standard
